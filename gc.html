Grenada's Culture

The language that is most spoken in Grenada is English. The most popular food include:

  • Callaloo Soup
  • Roti
  • Oil Down
  • Pelau

The people of Grenada wear red, yellow, and green colors. Popular music types in Grenada are calypso, soca and to a lesser extent reggae and dancehall. They dance dances like the Big drum dance, Belle dance and Quadrille dance.
