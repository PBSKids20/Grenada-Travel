Grenada's Climate & Wildlife

Grenada's Climate

The average temperature of Grenada is 80 Degrees F. The precipitation is about 78 inches annually. The humidity on average in Grenada is 74.% - 85%.

Grenada's Wildlife

The common plants you will see while visiting Grenada are the:

  • Seagrape
  • Avocado
  • Papaya

Common animals you will see include:

  • Common Tern
  • Yellow-Crowned Night Heron
  • Yellow Warbler

Some fish incude:

  • Great White Shark
  • Risso's Dolphin
  • Whale Shark
